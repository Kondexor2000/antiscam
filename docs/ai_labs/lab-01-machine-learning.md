# AI Lab 1: text classification

## Goal

Train and evaluate a simple text classifier for scam/safe messages.

## Tasks

1. Open `antiscam/ai.py`.
2. Train `NaiveBayesTextClassifier` on at least four labeled examples.
3. Predict the label for a new BLIK-related message.
4. Explain the role of token counts and Bayes scoring.

## Assessment

- 40% working classifier example,
- 30% interpretation of scores,
- 30% explanation of limitations compared with deep learning.
